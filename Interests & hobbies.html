Traveling

Saint Augustine once said, "The world is a book, and those who do not travel read only a page." More than any university subject, travel may educate you more. You gain knowledge of the culture of the country you are visiting. If you speak with locals, you will most likely discover their beliefs, customs, traditions, and history. Traveling may teach you a great deal about yourself. You can monitor how you feel while you are away from home. You'll discover how you feel about your home country. You'll realize how you feel about folks from other countries. You'll discover how much you know and how little you know about the world. You'll be able to watch how you react in unfamiliar situations. You'll be put to the test in terms of language, orientation, and social skills. After you come home, you will not be the same person.
