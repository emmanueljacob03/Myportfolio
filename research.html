A Research Investigation on M-Health Using AI and Big Data Analytics A Research Investigation on M-Health Using AI and Big Data Analytics International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews (IJRAR) · Oct 20, 2021International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews (IJRAR) · Oct 20, 2021 Show publication This peer-reviewed research paper presents a comprehensive exploration of how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Big Data Analytics can revolutionize mobile health (m-health) systems. It investigates how modern healthcare technologies, especially mobile-based devices, can be enhanced through intelligent data-driven systems to deliver predictive, personalized, and preventive healthcare solutions. Key Highlights: Reviewed and categorized unstructured healthcare data sources including EHRs, medical imaging, and clinical narratives, emphasizing the need for advanced analytics in m-health ecosystems. Evaluated AI-driven frameworks and data mining methodologies applied to real-time monitoring, diagnosis, and decision support systems. Introduced an AI-Big Data-based conceptual framework to handle large-scale health datasets efficiently, improving insights into patient behavior and care planning. Studied emerging technologies such as IoT, Bluetooth, GPS, and mobile sensors, and their role in remote patient monitoring, disease prediction, and automated health alerts. Discussed technical challenges including semantic reasoning, ontology integration, emotion recognition, and healthcare interoperability.

Tools & Topics Covered: AI Algorithms, Machine Learning, Big Data Frameworks, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Data Mining, Healthcare Informatics, Mobile App Analytics, Remote Monitoring Devices, Semantic Web Technologies, Smart Health Systems, Real-time Predictive Models.
